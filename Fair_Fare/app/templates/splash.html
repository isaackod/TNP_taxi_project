You're flying into Chicago and need to take a car to your hotel. Should you take a cab or download an app?

Using machine learning, Fair Fare will find the best ride for your route.

Go to app »

The Data

Fair fare combines two seperate datasets from the city of Chicago to get 100 million taxi and ridesharing trips. Each dataset is cleaned and saved in a common format. Real time traffic and routing information is obtained from the Google maps Directions API.

Taxi Dataset » Ridesharing Dataset » Maps API »

The Model

A subset of features suitble for use in a real-time application are used to to train a gradient boosted tree model for each dataset. The model has a MSE of 1$ on the taxi test set and a 2$ MSE for ridesharing trips.

View details »

The App

The webapp allows users to select a time and route. This information is geocoded and passed to the ML and aggregation pipleline to provide current price estimates, future price trands and price comparison to historical averages for similar routes.
